What Birth Injury Law Means

Common Birth Injury Cases We Handle

Cerebral Palsy (CP)

Often caused by oxygen deprivation (hypoxia) during delivery, requiring long-term care and therapy.

Brachial Plexus / Erb's Palsy

Nerve injuries from excessive traction during delivery (shoulder dystocia).

Hypoxic-Ischemic Encephalopathy (HIE)

Severe brain injury from inadequate oxygen or blood flow to the brain.

Birth Fractures & Nerve Damage

Broken bones or nerve injuries from mishandled delivery tools (forceps, vacuum).

Failure to Diagnose / Treat

Missed infections, unmanaged maternal conditions, or delayed C-sections that lead to harm.
