What Truck Accident Law Means

Truck accident claims typically involve serious injury, complex liability questions, and multiple insurance layers (driver, company, cargo owner). We investigate accident reports, black box data, driver logs, maintenance records, and CCTV to build a convincing case.

Common Types of Truck Cases

Jackknife & Rollover Crashes

Often caused by sharp maneuvers or cargo shift; very dangerous for nearby vehicles.

Blind-spot / Right-turn Collisions

Large trucks have significant blind spots; many crashes occur at intersections.

Fatigue / Hours-of-Service Violations

Driver log manipulation and fatigue are a frequent cause of truck collisions.

Overloaded / Improperly Secured Cargo

Shifting or falling cargo can cause loss of control or secondary accidents.

Commercial Vehicle Negligence

Manufacturer defects, poor company maintenance, or negligent hiring/training.
