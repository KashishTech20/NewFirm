What Is a Slip & Fall Accident?

Slip and fall accidents occur when an individual is injured due to hazardous conditions on someone elseâ€™s property. These cases fall under premises liability law, meaning property owners must maintain a safe environment for visitors. When they fail to do so and injuries occur, victims may be entitled to compensation.

Common Types of Slip & Fall Cases

  • Wet or slippery floors with no warning signs
  • Potholes or uneven walking surfaces
  • Poor lighting in hallways and staircases
  • Loose rugs, carpets, or mats
  • Broken stairs or missing handrails
  • Obstructions or clutter on walkways
  • Hazardous conditions in stores, malls, or restaurants
